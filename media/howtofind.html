How To Find a Member

Depending on what you are looking for, choose one of three starting places:

  1. Usually, you just want to find somebody, so use 'FIND'. Type in as much of their first (or last) name as you can remember. Upper or lower case does not matter. 'Jon' will get you 'J. Jones' and 'Joni Kletter', among others. If you are not too specific (like 'Xingtao') you'll get a listing of names within buttons. If you find the name you are looking for, just click on the button. Otherwise try again.
  2. If you've got two or three minutes to spare, you could fetch the entire Membership Directory. There is usually very little advantage in doing this unless you are almost completely at a lost on how to spell a person's name. From that rather long listing, click on individual names to get the complete details.
  3. The Pet Directory contains listings of creatures (mainly dogs) that own members. If you have a critter name or some FIDO dog tag identifying a critter, start there.), The Pet Directory lists associated members(s) with each pet; click on the member's name to get whatever contact information the database has on that person.), Just remember: we promise to keep ALL member information confidential. Please do not use this data for non-FIDO business or give this information to anyone who is not working on a FIDO project. Please protect your password to this web site. FIDO does not condone email spamming in any form; use the email links here sparingly and write to our members in a friendly way, as if their friendship matters, because it does.
