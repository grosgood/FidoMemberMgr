• GREETINGS •

This is a web application supporting the Membership Committee of FIDO in Prospect Park. If you are a member of this committee, or of the Steering Committee, have gotten this far but don't know how to proceed, then you should contact the Membership Chair by the usual means. If you are not a member of these committees, or are just generally interested in FIDO, you should go to the offical web site, FIDO in Prospect Park which has information about the organization, its purpose, events, and — if you are keen on preserving off-leash privileges for canines in Prospect Park, Brooklyn, New York — in joining our organization. Then, at some future date, you might re-visit this mundane, administrative web site as a volunteer of the Membership Committee, where the elite meet to eat, or, better, perhaps, to decode strangely written email addresses on membership forms.
